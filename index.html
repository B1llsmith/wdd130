Nairobi

Nairobi, the capital city of Kenya, is a vibrant and bustling metropolis that blends modernity with rich cultural heritage. Known as the "Green City in the Sun," it is surrounded by natural beauty, including the nearby Nairobi National Park, where wildlife roams against a backdrop of skyscrapers. As East Africa's economic hub, Nairobi boasts a thriving business district, world-class infrastructure, and a diverse population that contributes to its dynamic arts, music, and culinary scenes. The city's history is deeply tied to Kenya's independence movement, making it a cultural and political cornerstone in the region.
