Whitewater Wonders

Our Purpose and Mission

Our purpose is to provide safe, thrilling, and unforgettable whitewater rafting experiences. We are committed to sharing our passion for the outdoors, promoting environmental stewardship, and creating lasting memories for our guests. We believe in the power of adventure to connect people with nature and with each other.

History

Founded in 1998, Whitewater Wonders began as a small operation with a single raft and a dream. Our founder, an avid rafter, wanted to share the exhilarating beauty of the river with others. Over the decades, we've grown into a premier rafting outfitter known for our expert guides and commitment to safety.

From the very beginning, our philosophy has been to respect the river and its surrounding environment. We've been pioneers in eco-friendly rafting practices and continue to lead community efforts in river conservation, ensuring these natural wonders can be enjoyed for generations to come.
